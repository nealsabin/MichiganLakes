About this App

Scroll in for lakes and depth contours (where available) to appear. Clicking on a lake where there are depth contours avaible returns the approximate depth of the lake.

Clicking on a County returns various information about lakes within the County.

The accuracy and completeness of all data used is not guaranteed. GIS services are refereced from Michigan GIS Open Data.

This app was designed by Neal Sabin using the ArcGIS Javascript API. To download the project, click here.
